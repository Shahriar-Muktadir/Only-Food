Our Story

Our Restaurant is a small intimate BYOB located in a small rudimentary stone, colonial home dating back to 2010. Chef/Owner Yianni Arhontoulis serves Dinner Tuesday through Saturday. We offer creative yet approachable dinning, driven by each season’s bounty. Showcasing humble ingredients, served with the utmost love and care. The Restaurant seating 32 people is comprised of two small dining rooms. We have our larger main dining room with views of lovely cobblestoned Germantown avenue. As well as our smaller cozy library located in the back of the restaurant. During the Garden Districts warmer months we have an additional 14 seats available on our back patio for outdoor dining. We are incredibly adaptable given the small space and all allergies and aversions are accommodated with notice.
